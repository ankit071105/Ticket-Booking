Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the flight for a full refund.

How do I make changes to my booking?

You can make changes through our app or website by accessing your booking.

Can I change the date of my travel after booking?

Yes, you can change the travel date up to 48 hours before your flight.

Are there any additional fees not included in the booking price?

Additional fees such as luggage or in-flight services may apply.

What should I do if I miss my flight?

Please contact our support immediately for rebooking or further assistance.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, you can add travel insurance during the booking process.
