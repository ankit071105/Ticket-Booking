ATOL Protected - Your Holiday Security

At Ticket-Booking, Your holiday is protected by the ATOL scheme. This means your money is safeguarded, and you'll receive a refund if your travel company fails.

1. What is ATOL protection?

  • Financial Security: Your money is protected, ensuring you get a refund if your travel company goes bust.
  • Peace of Mind: Travel worry-free, knowing you're covered.
  • Expert Assistance: The Civil Aviation Authority (CAA) provides support and guidance.

2. How to Check if Your Booking is ATOL Protected:

  • Look for the ATOL Logo: This should be displayed on our website and in any booking confirmation documents.
  • Check Your ATOL Certificate: You will receive an ATOL Certificate after making a payment. This will confirm your protection and provide details of what to do if things go wrong.

3. Why ATOL Matters: Your Peace of Mind

ATOL (Air Travel Organiser's Licence) is a financial protection scheme that ensures your holiday is safeguarded. When you book a holiday with an ATOL-protected travel company, you can rest assured that:

  • Your Money is Safe: If your travel company goes out of business before or during your trip, ATOL will cover the cost of your return flight or refund the money you've paid.
  • You're Not Left Stranded: In the event of a company failure, ATOL will arrange for your return flight home, ensuring you're not left stranded in a foreign country.
  • You're Protected: ATOL provides peace of mind knowing that your holiday investment is secure.

For more information on ATOL protection, please visit: ATOL Website
