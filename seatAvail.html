Train And Bus Seat Availability

Whenever it’s travel time, everyone seems to go in overdrive for finding out train seat availability. So beat the crowd and book your ticket as early as possible. However, booking tickets in advance is a good option only if you are sure about your travel plans. For many travellers, this is often not the case.

Check Train And Bus Seat Availability Online

To check train seat availability, follow the steps mentioned below:

  1. Enter your Source and destination station.
  2. Choose the date of journey. You can also opt for Tatkal ticket booking.
  3. Search for trains, running dates, and seat availability.
  4. Choose your train and preferred class to complete your booking.

Train seat availability may differ based on the booking quota you select.

Train And Bus Seat Availability Search on IRCTC

One of the biggest concerns of train travellers is knowing about seat availability. With the train seat availability feature of Indian Railways on IRCTC website, one can easily check for any train and book tickets online.

Plan your trip 120 days in advance to get the best seat. For last-minute booking, Tatkal tickets are the best option.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q. How do I check my seat number on a train?

A: To check information about your seat, check your PNR status. This includes whether your ticket is confirmed, waitlisted (WL), or under reservation against cancellation (RAC).

Q. What does current seat availability mean?

A: It is the fastest way to find real-time booking information. Enter your From & To Station Name/Code along with travel dates and class to check.

Q. What are the chances of my ticket getting confirmed?

A: Waitlist prediction tools help you predict the chances of a waitlisted or RAC ticket getting confirmed based on trends.

Q. Can train seat availability change at the last minute?

A: Yes, availability can change. Chart preparation is done 4 hours before departure and again 30 minutes before departure, allowing seats to be freed up.
