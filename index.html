FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions.

How do I book a bus or train ticket on Ticket-Booking?

Booking a ticket is easy! Simply visit our website, select your preferred mode of travel (Bus or Train), enter your travel details (destination, date, and time), and proceed with booking. You'll receive real-time availability and secure payment options to complete your booking in just a few clicks.

Can I sell my bus or train tickets on Ticket-Booking?

Yes! If you have bus or train tickets you no longer need, you can sell them through Ticket-Booking. Contact our 24/7 customer support team to get the best price for your tickets without any hassle.

Is it safe to make payments on Ticket-Booking?

Absolutely! We prioritize the security of our users. Our platform uses secure payment gateways to ensure that your transactions are safe and encrypted, providing you with peace of mind during your booking process.

How do I check the availability of tickets?

You can check real-time availability by selecting your travel date and destination on our platform. Our system will show you all available options, allowing you to choose the best one for your journey.

What happens if I need to cancel or modify my booking?

If you need to cancel or modify your booking, please contact our customer support team. Cancellation and modification policies vary depending on the bus or train operator, so we’ll guide you through the process based on the terms and conditions of your specific booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular digital wallets, ensuring that you can pay using the option that suits you best.
